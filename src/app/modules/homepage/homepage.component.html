Welcome to Bluestore - Art Nirbhar Bazaar

#Thebluestore is your private shop on One Page Spotlight to list all your Art Products for sale to customers who are looking for you. TheBlueStore - Art Bazaar is a first of its kind virtual shopping festival where you search & discover art products and the artists behind them from across the country from niche to local discoveries. With a strong sentiment of Make In India, One Page Spotlight has endeavored to curate the best of the offerings from across 21 categories for you in these 28 days.
