About Saptarang

Saptarang at One Page Spotlight is an online concert featuring 24 musicians who will be performing from different parts of India. These 24 incredible artists will come together LIVE on Saturday, August 15th, 2020 to celebrate the spirit of India's 74th year of Independence.

Saptarang simply means seven colours which also highlights the seven scores of music. This diversity of colours describe the medley of a vibrant country like India which boasts of rich music from different cultures.These artists are geared to showcase their own compositions that display Indian classical music of hindustani and carnatic music along with the likes of contemporary tunes that is sure to appeal to India's modern generation as well.

24 Musicians

Vijay Prakash
Vijay Prakash is an Indian singer and music composer who has contributed to bollywood films such as Blue, Yuvvraaj, Swades, Kaal, Lakshya, Tere Naam, Cheeni Kum and many other regional films as well.
Sandeep Narayan
Sandeep Narayan is a classical trained musician from Chennai. He has completed concert tours of North America, the United Kingdom, Germany, Australia, Singapore, and Sri Lanka, and is regularly accompanied by leading artists in the field.
Manoj George
Manoj George is a violinist and composer who performed as a conductor, string arranger and choral arranger in the Grammy® award winning album “Winds of Samsara”. His wide repertoire includes scoring music for Malayalam and Kannada feature films, documentaries, short films and commercials.
Vijayaa Shanker
Trained in Carnatic Music under various eminent musicians of India, Vijayaa Shankar is a singer, songwriter, composer, voice coach, social worker and an entrepreneur who has sung in multilingual films and 2500 jingles and is a Sahitya Kala Parishad Award recipient.
Louis Banks
Known as the godfather of Jazz music in India, Louis Banks is a name that is synonymous with Jazz in a country of hindustani and carnatic music. He is a noted pianist who is one of the most sought after names in the music industry.
Giridhar Ghatam Udupa
Giridhar Udupa is a well-known percussionist who plays the Ghatam. With 3 decades of musical journey enriched by collaborations with eminent artists across the world, concert visits and numerous accolades, Udupa today is an ambassador of world music.
Vidushi Dr. Jayanthi Kumaresh
Dr. Jayanthi Kumaresh is India’s torchbearer worldwide for Saraswati Veena, the National Instrument of India. An artiste, collaborator, composer, creator, pioneer and educator for over 35 years, she truly embodies the rich musical heritage of India.
Vidwan R Kumaresh
Kumaresh is an icon of Carnatic Music and is also a child prodigy who began playing the violin from a young age. A lyricist and music director, he has been captivating people through his music for the last 4 decades with his extraordinary mastery.
Pandit Pravin Godkhindi
Pandit Pravin Godkhindi is one of India’s top fluatist. He is the only Indian Flautist to perform on the 8 Feet contrabass flute and has shared the stage with eminent artists like Ustad Zakir Hussain, Dr. Balmurli Krishna, Pt. Vishwa Mohat Bhatt and many others.
Gino Banks
Gino Banks is a Drummer and a member of the band ‘Nexus’. Gino is the son of legendary Jazz pianist, Louis Banks who started playing the drums at the age of 15 and like his father has shared the stage with music personalities.
Rakesh Chaurasia
Rakesh Chaurasia is an Indian flautist. He followed in the footsteps of his uncle, Pandit Hariprasad Chaurasia, the prolific flautist of all times. His versatility is known through his many collaborations of jazz and bollywood music.
Darshan Doshi
Darshan Doshi is a well-known Drummer in the music industry. Dashan has worked with the finest names like Ranjit Barot, Pankaj Sharma & Lester Godinho. He has played with bands like Farhan Live, Soul India with Hariharan, The Bombay Projekt, FUZONIKA, Mumbai Stomp with Taufiq Qureshi and many others.
Arun Kumar
A master of Carnatic percussion, Arun Kumar has been in the field of music since 1986 and is a famous jazz Drummer. He renders percussion support to carnatic classical, talavadya, jazz music, light music and dance performances.
Sheldon D’silva
Sheldon D’silva is a bass guitarist, music producer and composer based in Mumbai who has had the experience of playing his six string bass guitar with some of the finest names like Pandit Vishwa Mohan Bhatt, Taufiq Qureshi, Shankar Mahadevan and many more noteworthy musicians.
Vidwan Jayachandra Rao
K.U Jayachandra Rao is an eminent Mridangam artist whose work collaborations have won him several accolades over the years and he continues to perform all across the country and impart his valuable knowledge with young artists globally.
Kiran Godkhindi
Kiran Godkhindi is a noted tabla player who belongs to a family of established musicians namely Pt. Venkatesh Godkhindi and Pt. Pravin Godkhindi, his father and brother. Together, they have given several performances across the country.
Stephen Devassy
Stephen Devassy is a pianist, composer and a music producer who is currently the youth icon of modern music. He is an exemplary musician in electronic and acoustic sounds and has explored various styles like classical, blues, classic rock, jazz, Carnatic and world music fusion.
Srinivas Achar
Srinivas Achar is a guitarist from Bangalore who has shared the stage with eminent musicians in the music industry like Smt.Chandrika Gururaj, Smt Ratnamala Prakash, Shri Puttur Narasimha Nayak, Shir Srinivas Udupa and many more.
V Umesh
V Umesh is an accomplished keyboard player, compere and also a director who has played in various concerts where the giants of music like P.B.S, Janaki amma, Praveen Ghodkindi, Shivamani and others. He has worked in the field of Kannada Movies as well.
Vidwan Pramath Kiran
Pramath Kiran is India’s most versatile percussion artist who plays an array of instruments like Tabla and Morsing and has special interest in Afro-cuban styles and latin percussion.
Varun Pradeep
Varun Pradeep is a keyboard player based in Bangalore. He has been a part of several concerts and played alongside popular drummer Arun Kumar, singer Vijay Prakash and many other names in the music industry.
Bruthuva Caleb
Bruthva is a bass guitarist, pianist and a choir conductor based in Bangalore. He has been a part of several concerts and played alongside popular drummer Arun Kumar, singer Vijay Prakash and many other names in the music industry.
Pradyumna Soraba
Pradyumna Soraba is a Tabla player based in Bangalore who has shared the stage with artists like Pandit Pravin Godkindi and Gino Banks.
Shadaj Godkhindi
Shadaj Godkhindi is an upcoming Hindustani classical fluatist. A young and talented musician as Shadaj hails from a family of musicians and has performed several times along with his father Pandit Pravin Godkhindi and uncle Kiran Godkhindi.
