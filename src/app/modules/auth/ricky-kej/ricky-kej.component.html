About Ricky Kej LIVE

Ricky Kej LIVE at One Page Spotlight,
is a concert featuring over 40 musicians from 6 countries
including 5 Grammy® Award Winners.
This concert on Earth Day 2020 (22nd April)
will be releavent to the current situation, and about
"Healthy People and a Healthy Planet".
Musicians from across the globe have been chosen to
promote a message of Solidarity.
All the musicians will be performing from their respective homes
to encourage
#StayHome and #StaySafe,

Artist Ensemble

Ricky Kej
Grammy® Award Winner, US Billboard #1 artist, UNESCO mgiep , Ambassador & UNICEF ‘Celebrity Supporter' Ricky Kej is an internationally renowned Indian Music Composer, Environmentalist and Professor who have won more than a 100 music awards in over 20 countries.
Lonnie Park
A Grammy® Nominee vocalist, Keyboardist, songwriter, engineer, producer, and, Lonnie Park is the lead singer of the band Ten Man Push. He actively produces commissioned music for causes such as Autism Works, Autism Movement, Puzzle Solvers.
Baaba Maal
Baaba Maal, a Senegalese singer and guitarist born in Podor, is a well known African and international musician who plays acoustic guitar, and percussion and has released 11 studio albums and voiced the Wakandan soundtrack of the blockbuster film 'Black Panther'.
Laura Dickinson
Grammy® Winner from USA, Laura is a hugely popular vocal contractor and voice actress in Hollywood, whose vocals can be heard in “Rogue One: A Star Wars Story”, “Supernatural” to name a few and is known for infusing a unique freedom and laser- beam vocal precision into every song she performs.
Wouter Kellerman
A Grammy® Award-winning South African flutist, producer and composer, Wouter won six South African Music Awards and has focused his attention on World and Roots music, exploring the versatility of the instrument and fusing classical and contemporary sounds.
Mzansi Youth Choir
The Mzansi Youth Choir is globally recognised as Africa's finest show choir and the only choir from the African continent to be selected to perform at the International Youth Choir Festival at the Royal Albert Hall, London in April 2017.
Pt. Vishwa Mohan Bhatt
Grammy® Winning Hindustani classical music instrumentalist and Creator of the MOHAN VEENA (slide guitar), Vishwa Mohan Bhatt has mesmerized the world with his pristine pure, delicate yet fiery music and his perfect assimilation of sitar, sarod & Veena techniques.
IP Singh
Popularly known for being the lead vocalist of the band 'Fardikot', IP Singh has been in the music scene as a composer, lyricist and singer for the past 11 years with 500 shows under his belt.
Varijashree Venugopal
An award winning vocalist and flute player, Varijashree is known for her immense virtuosity as a cross-cultural musician and performer. She was found to have the rare ability of identifying about 200 ragas at the age of four years.
Hai Phuong
Hai Phuong, one of the leading Dan Tranh musicians in the world has won 'The Best Contemporary Music Performer Prize' in 'The First National Concours of Young Zither talents 1992'. Currently, Hai Phuong is a teacher of Tranh Zither in HCMC conservatory.
Manoj George
Manoj George is a violinist and composer who performed as a conductor, string arranger and choral arranger in the Grammy® award winning album “Winds of Samsara”. His wide repertoire include scoring music for Malayalam and Kannada feature films, documentaries, short films and commercials.
Arun Kumar
A master of Carnatic percussion, Arun Kumar has been in the field of Music since 1986 and is a famous jazz Drummer. He renders percussion support to Carnatic Classical, Talavadya, Jazz music, Light Music and Dance Performances.
Vijayaa Shanker
Trained in Carnatic Music under various eminent musicians of India, Vijayaa Shankar is a singer songwriter, composer, voice coach who has sung in multi-lingual films and 2500 jingles and is Sahitya Kala Parishad Award recipient.
The Raveolution Choir
Conducted by Dominic D'Cruz, the Raveolution Choir is a collection of few of the best singers and musicians from Bengaluru. The choir is versatile and has performed at several concerts with Grammy® Award Winner Ricky Kej.
Vilas Nayak
Vilas Nayak, a self-taught speed painting artists widely recognized by his performances on 'Asia's Got Talent' on AXN and 'Got Talent World Stage Live'. He has performed in over 28 countries on some of the most prestigious stages.
